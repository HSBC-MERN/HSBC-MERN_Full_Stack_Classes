Color Values: 
--------------
    1. color name
    2. rgb(red, green , blue)    ->0-255
    3. rgba(red, green, blue, alpha)   -> 0-255     alpha -> 0-1 
    4. hsl(hue , saturation , lightless)  ->hue ->0- 360 deg    s, l-> 0% - 100%
    5. hsla(hue , saturation , lightless, alpha)  ->hue ->0- 360 deg    s, l-> 0% - 100%
    6. hexa-code number->0 -9 alp ->a, f
    7. transparent
